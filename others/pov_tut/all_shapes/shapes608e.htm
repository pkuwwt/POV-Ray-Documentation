Unicode and Asian Character Sets

To render Asistic, Indian and Arabic types of characters,we need to have the according unicode fonts installed.
(look for the "far easter language support" of Microsoft!)
If we live right in these regions some of them are just installed.

Some famous Unicode-Sets are:
ttf "MSMINCHO.TTF""
ttf "SIMSUN.TTF"
ttf "BATANG.TTF"
ttf "ARIALUNI.TTF"
