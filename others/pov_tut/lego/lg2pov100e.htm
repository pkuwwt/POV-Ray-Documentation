CAD Programs for virtual LEGO® Models

LEGO Digital Designer ® (Windows PC and Mac OSX)

- the origial CAD program from LEGO® - freeware
A very usful toole with many samples also for the LEGO Mindstorms system (robots etc.).
Homepage of the LDD : LEGO Digital Designer ®
LDraw is a freeware CAD Program Base (Windows Macintosh Linux)
with a bunch of Font-Ends and Add ons like LeoCAD, MLCad, L3Lab, L3PAO, LDview, LPub.
Homepage of LDraw (en): www.LDraw.org

LDraw with LeoCAD (Windows + Linux)

LeoCAD - a CAD font end for LDraw (Windows + Linux).
Homepage of LeoCAD (en): www.leocad.org

LDraw with MLCad (Windows)

MLCad comes also with the LDraw package (Windows).
Another famous solution for a CAD font end for LDraw
Homepage of MLCad (en/de): www.lm-software.com/mlcad/
