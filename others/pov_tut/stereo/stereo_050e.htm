Cross-Eye View
Viewing stereo images without additional helps, even when wearing glasses.
How to view the stereoscopic effect
in image pairs for cross-eye view :
For this it's important to not tilt the head and to look vertically to the screen.
The images here above represent an cross-eye image pair.
We put a finger right between these two images and our eyes.
While focusing the tip of our finger we can notice that, in the background, the
"+" is moving inside the "o".
We will perceive three images like the images below. (with 4 image we overdo!)
We have to concentrate our attention on the middle and
ignore the other blured images right and left of the center.
We can use our flat hands as blinders to blend out the iritating goast images
right and left of the center.
